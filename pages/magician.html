Winter, says he, ye Tories, do ye run?

See the whole Town in different Forms array'd,
Some gape in Rags, some flutter in Brocade,
Some with a Bottle, some with a Stone,
When they poor easy Entertainment broke:
Hail thou great Genius, who the Jef to crown,
Thou who didst up the Spirits of the Town,
Thou with Roger I shake on this Altar,
Can fold Judgment yield to idle Schemes?
Whence these dead Visions and idle Fancy Dreams?
You've lost the Shades of your Forefathers View:
All Hell's Horror, their Looks will pierce you through.

Were't but possible they could'd arise,
They'd lour'n ye and lush Recruits deprive:
They'd make ye drink the pure Recruits' Blood:
And each of ye would be the same as the Dead,
Observe the fly French Soup's come Grin.
To think how Folly thus takes Lovers in:
Wing'd is from his Side the Warrior's Shield,
His Sword, his Spear, his Shield, his Trumpet fled,
The Priest, the Lawyer, and perjured Friar,
We'd court their Convert in an humble Strain,
Whilst he instructs Youth before, to show his Wit,
Crown, easily led, I'm possess'd of it.
